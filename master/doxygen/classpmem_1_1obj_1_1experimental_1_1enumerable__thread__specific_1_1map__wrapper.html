template<typename T, template< typename... > class Map = std::unordered_map, typename Mutex = pmem::obj::shared_mutex, typename Storage = segment_vector<T>>
class pmem::obj::experimental::enumerable_thread_specific< T, Map, Mutex, Storage >::map_wrapper

Private helper wrapper class for map_type.

Needed to construct map_type through v<>.get().

We can't use v<map_type *> because in case of crash and reboot: enumerable_thread_specific's constructor will not be called, v<>.get() will construct pointer, not the map, and thread mapping will not restore.
